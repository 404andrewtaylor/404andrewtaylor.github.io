Research

Convolutional PCR diagnostics

  • Pathogen identification from mixed biological samples using many short primers to generate a complex genomic signal that is deconvoluted with AI, a low-cost, high-information alternative to narrow single-target tests
  • PCR and ultra-fast qPCR assay development: primer design, multiplexed panel layout, SYBR Green and probe-based chemistries, universal-tail primer systems
  • Wet-lab validation: DNA extraction and purification, gel electrophoresis, 16S and pathogen-panel amplification, primer dropout experiments, replicate analysis
  • qPCR instrument analysis: amplification curves, Ct calling, melt-curve derivative peak identification, and alignment of experimental results with computational predictions
  • Short-primer feasibility studies, dimer and secondary-structure screening, GC content and homopolymer quality control
  • 96-well plate design and multi-channel fluorescence readout on rapid-cycling thermal hardware
  • Bioinformatics for assay design: reference genome panel curation, in-silico amplicon prediction, and large-scale primer candidate evaluation
  • Custom PCB thermal cyclers with trace-routed heating elements for rapid nucleic acid cycling

Custom CPAP manufacturing

  • Print-farm and slicing automation for Vitacore's custom CPAP interface manufacturing, supporting production of tailored masks from digital designs through to printed parts
  • Automated baseplate generation, slicing pipelines, and print-job orchestration for medical-device fabrication at scale

Algae cultivation & carbon isotope separation

  • Algae-based carbon-12 / carbon-13 separation approach as an alternative to conventional carbon monoxide distillation, aimed at reducing capital and operating cost of isotope separation
  • Large-scale automated microalgae cultivation apparatus: custom fluidic routing with membrane interfaces, watertight connection engineering across multiple hardware revisions, and pressure management across multi-chamber circuits
  • 48 peristaltic pumps across six ESP32 controller boards; custom motherboard and daughterboard PCBs with MOSFET motor drivers and pressure sensing
  • FFT-based frequency-domain RPM stabilization for simultaneous multi-motor control, scaled from single-motor proof-of-concept to seven motors running concurrently; diagnosed and resolved PWM signal interference limiting reliable operation to six motors per board
  • Cloud-deployed workflow automation (Flask, WebSockets, Docker, Google Cloud Run): pre-built sequences from minutes to 16+ hours, real-time progress tracking, motor command verification with retry logic, emergency stop, and workflow cancellation
  • Centralized motor mapping across functional subsystems (CO2 transfer, O2 stripping, buffer circulation, algae circuits, and carbonate dosing) with configurable custom workflow composition
  • Microalgae cultivation (Chlorella vulgaris, Spirulina): culture collection, pasteurization, fermentation, harvest, and growth-media optimization
  • Drosophila feeding trials on fermented algal diets; sustained viable colonies through third generation on fermented Chlorella
  • Glucose and metabolite extraction research from algal biomass (enzymatic hydrolysis pathways)

3D tissue culture & structured biological manufacturing

  • Computationally designed vascular structures for hydrogel tissue blocks: low-cost gelatin-based materials and workflows that form channels and support perfused 3D tissue culture
  • STL-to-vasculature modeling: generating 3D vascular networks inside arbitrary volumes for tissue-scaffold applications
  • Clustering-based and space-colonization algorithms for realistic vessel branching, with MST routing and intersection avoidance

Fluorescent oxygen sensing

  • Fluorescence-lifetime-based oxygen sensors built around low-power integrated electronics and oxygen-sensitive dyes
  • PIC microcontroller firmware, I2C sensor communication, and calibration sweeps for compact oxygen instrumentation
  • Aimed at demanding applications such as anesthesia and rebreathers, where reliable oxygen measurement matters

Plastic identification & robotic separation

  • Automated plastic sorting platform combining robotics, spectroscopy, and machine perception
  • Near-infrared identification with focused illumination, scanning detector architecture, and RGB imaging for classifying plastics in motion
  • Custom PCB design for multi-spectral LED arrays and sensor readout; reflectivity simulation and benchtop validation
  • Tile-based actuation hardware for physical material handling
